Technical Project Manager

Kowloon Tong, Kowloon, HKSAR, China

We are currently seeking a technical project manager to join our team.

Primary responsibilities:

  • Oversee various projects in the educational, financial, health, legal, professional services, and public sectors in Hong Kong and around the world
  • Manage small- and medium-sized technical and non-technical working groups to ensure that projects are delivered on-time and to-specifications
  • Manage expectations of all stakeholders relevant to the organisation and projects

Qualifications:

  • Must be hungry to learn about blockchain solution design
  • Must be fluent in English (Cantonese and/or Mandarin is a bonus)
  • Must have discipline in time management and interpersonal communication
  • Preferably familiar with project management methodologies, such as waterfall, agile, scrum, kanban, XP, critical path method, etc.
  • Preferably proficient with project management tools, such as Jira, Trello, Gantt charts, etc.
  • Note: knowledge of relevant industries and sectors gives you extra credits

Perks:

  • Training and certification in blockchain.
  • Free drinks and meals provided on an irregular basis.
