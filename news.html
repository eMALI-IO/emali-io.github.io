EMALI a winner of Best Digital Identity Solution Award in HKMA and BIS Trade Finance Challenge

EMALI is a winner of the Best Digital Identity Solution Award in the HKMA and BIS Trade Finance Challenge. This challenge is a joint initiative of the BIS Innovation Hub (BISIH) and the Hong Kong Monetary Authority (HKMA), and is designed to showcase the potential for new innovative technologies to resolve problems in trade finance (TradeTech).
