About EMALI

EMALI is founded by Dr. Lawrence Ma and Peter Woo in 2016. Together with our clients, we focus on re-imagining fundamental business transactions, inventing new interactions and creating streamlining process that establish trust, accountability and transparency.

We help enterprises leverage financial technology to deliver maximum value to their business through AI, Blockchain, Cryptography and Privacy.

We are currently working on various fintech software and solutions specializing on identity, banking, insurance claims, fraud detection and regulatory registries.
